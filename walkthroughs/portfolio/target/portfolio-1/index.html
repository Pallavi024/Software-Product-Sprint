Happy Menses

Menstruation is one of the biggest taboo in India, to overcome it and promote menstrual hygiene, I along with my team designed an android application with the following features :-

  • Cycle tracker – To keep track of menstrual cycle and give timely reminders
  • Shopping cart – A place to buy all the menstrual products and the payment can be done using PayPal
  • Modules – To give information about various menstrual disorders, myths and more.
